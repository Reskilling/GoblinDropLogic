How to Import Your Bank to Gearscape

01

Install Bank Memory Plugin

Open RuneLite Plugin Hub, search “Bank Memory” & click Install.

02

Prepare Your Bank

Deposit all worn gear. Open your bank, click the Bank Memory icon (right side of Runelite), go to Saved Banks, and right-click your bank → Copy item data to clipboard.

03

Open Gearscape

Navigate to Gearscape.net

04

Pull Stats

Go to Best Setups, enter your character name and click the blue Wise Old Man icon.

05

Import Bank Data

Under “Select items to”, choose include and make sure "Only include selected items" is toggled on. Then click the RuneLite icon, select Bank Memory, and paste your clipboard contents.

06

Configure Setup

Add the boss or mob you want to calculate for. Click the cog icon (top-right) to confirm advanced settings.

07

Run the Calculator

Click Best Setup. Gearscape will generate the optimal gear using your specific bank items.

Stuck? Ensure gear is deposited before copying. If issues persist, ask in Discord for help.

Common Skill Grinds

Skill Training Guides

Detailed guidance on skill training for every Ironman stage.

Crafting

The seaweed-and-sand method is the most sustainable:

  • • Obtain seaweed spores by killing Lobstrosities or waiting for natural spawns.
  • • Plant Seaweed and between each growth cycle:
    • - Mine sand
    • - Convert the sand into molten glass (using the glassmake spell or a furnace)
    • - Blow the molten glass on the island above the underwater area on Fossil Island

Herblore

  • • Complete daily farming contracts.
  • • Always grow herbs and secondary ingredients.
  • • Early bosses like Ice King and Moons of Peril drop useful seeds.
  • • Set Kingdom of Miscellania to herb.
  • • Herblore is a slow progression skill and should not be neglected.

PvM Progression Path

Recommended progression route:

  • • Morytania Hard Diary
  • • Medium Combat Achievements

Start with Barrows for tank gear. Progress to Moons of Peril, then Royal Titans. This provides gear suitable for nearly all PvM content.

Slayer Strategy

Prioritize point boosting early in your Slayer journey:

  • • Unlocks access to superior monsters.
  • • Allows skipping undesirable tasks.
  • • Enables purchase of useful Slayer unlocks.
  • • Prevents wasting time and resources on inefficient tasks later.
Slayer Task Summary

Slayer Efficiency Upgrades (via Last Man Standing)

These upgrades support early Slayer progression while saving points and gp:

Rune Pouch

  • - Cost: 750 Slayer points from any Slayer master
  • - Alternative: 75 points from Last Man Standing
  • - Alternative: 150 telekinetic, 150 graveyard, 1500 enchantment, and 200 alchemist pizazz points from MTA

Rune Arrows

Available at x300 arrows for 3 points from LMS. Useful source of early Ranged ammo for Ironman without spending gp.

Item Imbuing

Use PvP Arena to earn Scrolls of Imbuing:

  • • Participation or leaving matches earns points.
  • • One scroll takes under an hour to obtain.
  • • Scrolls are reusable and can be removed from items.
  • • Dying in the Wilderness with a scroll-imbued item grants 650,000 Nightmare Zone points.

Prayer Supplies from Varlamore

At 75 Hunter, catch Moonlight Moths:

  • • Base version provides 1 dose of prayer potion.
  • • Adding certain meats to the moths yields 2-dose versions.
  • • High catch rate allows consistent protection prayer usage without relying on ranarr drops.
