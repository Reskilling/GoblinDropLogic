How to Import Your Bank to Gearscape

01

Install Bank Memory Plugin

Open RuneLite Plugin Hub, search “Bank Memory” & click Install.

02

Prepare Your Bank

Deposit all worn gear. Open your bank, click the Bank Memory icon, go to Saved Banks, and right-click your bank → Copy item data to clipboard.

03

Open Gearscape

Navigate to Gearscape.net

04

Pull Stats

Go to Best Setups, enter your character name and click the blue Wise Old Man icon.

05

Import Bank Data

Under “Select items to”, choose include and make sure "Only include selected items" is toggled on. Then click the RuneLite icon, select Bank Memory, and paste your clipboard contents.

06

Configure Setup

Add the boss or mob you want to calculate for. Click the cog icon (top-right) to confirm advanced settings.

07

Run the Calculator

Click Best Setup. Gearscape will generate the optimal gear using your specific bank items.

Stuck? Ensure gear is deposited before copying. If issues persist, ask in Discord for help.

Standard Operating Procedures

Skilling

  • Skill Training Guides - Detailed guidance on skill training for every Ironman stage.

Crafting: Seaweed/Mining Sand Loop

Sustainable Loop: Obtain Spores (Lobstrosities/Spawns) → Plant Seaweed → Mine Sandstone → Superglass Make (Spell/Furnace) → Blow Glass (Fossil Island Workstation).

Herblore: Consistent Sustain

Sustain Sequence: Farming Contracts → Grow Herbs & Secondaries → Boss Seeds (Ice King/Moons) → Kingdom to Herbs → Consistent Daily Progression.

* Note: Herblore is a slow progression skill and should not be neglected.

Combat & Slayer Strategy

PvM Progression Path

Deployment Sequence: Morytania Hard Diary → Medium Combat Achievements → Barrows (Tank Gear) → Moons of Peril (Mid-tier sets) → Royal Titans.

  • Combat Achievement Strategies - Simplified pathing for tier rewards.

Slayer Optimization

Strategic Sequence: Early Point Boosting → Superior Monsters → Task Skips/Blocks → Essential Unlocks → Long-term Efficiency.

Prioritize Point Boosting early to secure:

  • • Immediate access to Superior Slayer Monsters for unique loot.
  • • Capability to skip undesirable or inefficient tasks as they appear.
  • • Faster path to essential unlocks (Slayer Helm, Broad Fletching, Slayer Ring).
  • • Prevention of wasted time and supply resources on low-efficiency tasks.
  • Ironman Slayer Task Summary - Detailed guidance on tasks to complete, skip, or block.

Item Upgrades

Last Man Standing & Mage Training Arena Efficiency Upgrades

Rune Pouch Acquisition:

  • • 750 Slayer points from any Slayer master.
  • • 75 points from Last Man Standing (LMS).
  • • 150 Telekinetic, 150 Graveyard, 1500 Enchantment, and 200 Alchemist Pizazz points from MTA.

Rune Arrows: Available at x300 arrows for 3 points from LMS. High-value early Ranged ammo for zero GP.

Item Imbuing (Emir's Arena)

  • • Earn Scrolls of Imbuing via participation (playing or leaving earns points).
  • • Speed: One scroll typically takes under one hour to obtain.
  • • Utility: Scrolls are reusable and can be removed/transferred between items.
  • • Wilderness Meta: Dying in the Wildy with a scroll-imbued item grants 650,000 NMZ points.
